Welcome to NexusAI

Let's get your installation up and running in just a few steps.

NexusAI is a modular, distributed platform for orchestrating large language models, workers, and bots. Here's what you can do with it:

  • [1] Orchestrate LLMs — route prompts to local or cloud models (Ollama, OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, and more).
  • [2] Manage Workers — register GPU / CPU worker agents and monitor their load in real time.
  • [3] Deploy Bots — configure intelligent bots with custom roles, priorities, and backend chains.
  • [4] Track Tasks — observe queued, running, and completed inference tasks from one dashboard.
